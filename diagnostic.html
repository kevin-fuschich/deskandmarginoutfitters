Reinforcement Diagnostic

A quick assessment of alignment stability across leadership systems and cultural signals. Answer all nine questions, then calculate your score.

9 questions · ~2 minutes · self-administered

Core Conditions

Foundational conditions that determine whether leadership intent can travel consistently.

1.

Trust Alignment — senior leaders trust that strategic priorities are interpreted consistently across teams.

2.

Strategic Clarity — strategic priorities are clearly understood across functions and levels.

3.

Cross-Functional Cohesion — functions operate as coordinated partners rather than independent actors.

System Layer

The translation engine: narrative, cadence, and decision signals.

4.

Narrative Consistency — leaders describe strategy and priorities using consistent language.

5.

Operating Rhythm Alignment — routines and cadences reinforce strategic priorities.

6.

Decision Signal Clarity — important decisions reinforce the same priorities leaders communicate.

Field Signals

Visible reinforcement: behavior, rituals, and artifacts.

7.

Leadership Behavior Consistency — leadership actions reflect stated expectations.

8.

Ritual Reinforcement — recurring rituals reinforce strategic priorities.

9.

Artifact Alignment — messages, programs, and symbols reinforce priorities without mixed signals.

When all questions are scored, calculate your Reinforcement Stability Score.
