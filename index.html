The problem

Growth, acquisition, leadership turnover, restructuring, remote shifts — these moments create noise. Without intentional communication architecture:

  • Alignment fractures.
  • Informal narratives take over.
  • High performers disengage.
  • Trust erodes quietly.

Most organizations focus on operational integration. Few protect cultural integration.

What we do

Desk & Margin partners with executive teams to design structured, human-centered communication systems that stabilize organizations during change.

  • Executive narrative clarity
  • Transition communication architecture
  • Engagement rhythm design
  • Recognition system alignment
  • Post-survey activation strategy

We do not create noise. We create coherence.

Engagement model

Every partnership begins with Desk & Margin Discovery — a structured assessment designed to surface friction, risk points, and opportunity areas during transition.

1. Strategic Advisory Executive alignment and narrative design.
2. Systems Build Communication cadence, rituals, and engagement frameworks.
3. Activation Support Rollout, reinforcement, and leader enablement.

High-touch. Limited engagements. Focused scope.

Who we work with

  • Private equity-backed firms navigating integration
  • Enterprise teams recalibrating after growth or restructuring
  • Leadership groups seeking cultural durability
