Why it matters

Culture is decision quality at scale.

Culture determines how decisions get made when leadership is not in the room. Under strain — growth, leadership change, integration, distributed work — alignment often begins drifting long before anyone notices. When drift compounds, execution slows, friction rises, trust weakens, and innovation gets conservative.

Surveys can reveal symptoms. Reinforcement architecture fixes causes.
